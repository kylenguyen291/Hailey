From the quiet stillness of the universe, a lonely planet emerged - and with it, life itself, endlessly glooming and blooming in every breath of time…
When shooting stars sweep across the night sky, the flowers below awaken, glowing softly as if answering their light - each bloom unfolding in conjunction with every falling star…
And just like that, twenty years ago, a very special flower opened its first petals beneath that starlit sky…
Nurtured by warmth, challenges, laughter, and dreams, she grew — delicate yet resilient, gentle yet full of strength…
And now, she stands more radiant and glowing than ever, a rare blossom whose beauty outshines even the constellations above…
But sometimes, the paths ahead are not as simple as they seem — and now, the journey goes on, as she searches for the hidden answer waiting just beyond the stars…
